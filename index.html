Frequently asked questions

How do I book an online consultation?

Simply click the 'Book Consultation Online' button on our website. You can select your preferred date, time, and legal area directly from our calendar.

What should I prepare for my initial consultation?

Gather any relevant documents, notes, or timelines related to your legal matter. A clear understanding of your situation helps us provide the best advice.

What areas of law does Veritas Legal Group specialize in?

We primarily focus on business law, contract disputes, intellectual property, and civil litigation. Please see our services page for more details.

How are legal fees structured?

Our fees are transparent and discussed upfront. We offer various arrangements, including flat fees for certain services and hourly rates, depending on the complexity.

Can I meet with a lawyer in person?

While we prioritize convenient online services, in-person meetings can be arranged if necessary. Please discuss this during your initial consultation.
